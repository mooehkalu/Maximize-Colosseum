Welcome to the Nerd Fitness Rebellion! Here are a few quick steps to help you get started

1

CREATE YOUR CHARACTER

When you close out this window, you’ll be on your Character Page. Click on the pencil in the upper righthand corner to create your character name, backstory, goals, slogan, and update your profile photo. You can change any of this at any time, so don’t worry about getting them perfect!

2

SELECT A CLASS

Everyone joining the Nerd Fitness Rebellion, begins simply as a “Rebel.” Based on your goals, you can join one of seven classes. The full class descriptions can be found here. And don’t worry, you can change your class at any time. Plus, if you aren’t interested in picking a class just yet, that’s fine too! You’ll remain a Rebel until you want to specialize.

3

CREATE YOUR EPIC QUEST

In the navigation menu to your left, there is a “My Epic Quest” section (it’s the first one!). Click here and you can start creating your epic quest list. There you can create quests and quest categories, and assign XP (experience) to each quest. As you check them off, you’ll level up!
